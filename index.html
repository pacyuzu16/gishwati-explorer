About this entry

Gishwati-Mukura National Park collapsed to ~600 ha by 2002 and is now recovering — a local restoration story feeding the global Kunming-Montreal Global Biodiversity Framework.

Data sources 2+ required ✓

  • Rwanda land cover — RCMRD / RCoE Geoportal

Data is real: WDPA boundary + Hansen Global Forest Change (263 loss polygons, 2001–2023) clipped to the park in Google Earth Engine. Forest cover 2000: 2,810 ha · loss: 309 ha.
